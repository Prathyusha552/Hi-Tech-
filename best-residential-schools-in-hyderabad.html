Residential Dormitories

The residential facilities are of high standard. Accommodation is provided in comfortable halls with adequate and independent storage space. Separate accommodation for boys and girls are provided, ensuring privacy and freedom. The hostel rules are meticulously followed to ensure security and welfare of the students.

Moreover, personnel in charge of hostels, blocks and tutorial staff stay in the hostel premises along with the students to provide ready assistance 24/7.

Dining Hall

Nutritious and satisfying food is vital for growing children and intellect develops on healthy food. Keeping this in view the school maintains spacious dining halls and well equipped culinary arrangements.

Food is prepared under conditions of scrupulous cleanliness and supervision. Nutritious and adequate, it is steam cooked and satisfies every plate.

Menu is so planned as to provide variety and promote health. The faculty and staff share the board with the students to create a family-like atmosphere.

Mineral water supply

Pure and clean drinking water is made available to the students in the residential campus. We are having our own mineral water plant. Water purified through imported technology is made available to our students.

Power Backup

We have a generator to ensure that there is power back-up at all times. We believe, these basic necessities play a big role in providing a comfortable residential experience to our students.

Health care

Health care of students is given top priority in our campus. An in-house dispensary is kept open at all times to attend emergency needs. Parents are informed if the problem is serious. In the residential campus, doctor visits daily to look after the health needs of students.

If necessary, the problems are referred to specialists, Children are transferred to a city hospital under specialists care in case of any serious problem. A qualified staff nurse stays in the campus to attend on sick students.

Solar water heating system for entire campus

We have an eco friendly way of providing hot water to our students and staff. The solar water heating plant that we have installed ensures hot water to the entire campus without heating the environment.

Library

A library is a source of information. The habit of reading has to be cultivated from childhood itself. Acquisition of knowledge is a continuous process. At a later stage of life reading will prove to be a source of happiness.

Our library is extensive, well stocked with latest books and journals and currently needed information. The student can profitably browse, do serious research and read undisturbed in the cozy atmosphere.

Auditorium

We have a 6,000 square feet auditorium in the campus to conduct various functions and celebrations like the convocation, farewell, etc. The auditorium is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities like a projector, adequate lights, mics, speakers, etc

Outdoor study area

We have a dedicated outdoor study area for the students where they can sit amidst nature and study. The outdoor area has adequate greenery to calm the minds of the students and to help them concentrate better.

Kala Bhavan

The Kala Bhavan is the cultural hub of the school. It literally sets the stage for our students to bring out their talents and to make them more confident individuals.

Classrooms

Hi-Tech Education understands the importance of environment of learning. Pleasant class rooms are essential to make children alert and receptive. To this end, class rooms at Hi-Tech School are large, bright and spacious. Comfortable furniture for individual and group work is available. Wide and soft wall boards make learning a pleasure.

Laboratories for practicals

The laboratory work in physical and life sciences is designed to promote curiosity in children. Hence every effort is made to develop skills in the various fields. Care is taken to harness and not to suppress the natural instinct of inquisitiveness of the child. Thus, we have an updated and well-equipped Chemistry, Biology and Physics Lab.

Computer Lab

In the age of IT and AI, computers have become a necessity. To cater this need, the school has fully air-conditioned and user friendly computer labs. These labs are well equipped with latest technology DELL PCs and a network binding them together. Internet facility with broadband connection paves the way for the students to become highly skilled resource persons and technocrats in the near future.

These student laboratories support the academic study of Computer Science as a subject. The highly qualified teachers bring about this new confidence in the students by making them ‘tech savvy’.

Swimming pool

We have a well-maintained swimming pool with a coach to help our young ones learn this important life skill. The pool is frequently cleaned and utmost hygiene and safety standards are maintained.

Basketball Court

The Basketball Court is among the most favorite spots of the students. We believe physical activities are very crucial to a healthy mind. Thus we focus on including such sports in the schedule of the students.

Throwball Court

We have a dedicated Throwball court to let the kids indulge in some high-energy sports. This is a part of our endeavor to provide holistic development to the kids.

Volley Ball Court

Playing Volley Ball is a great stress buster. We have a Volley Ball Court with a qualified instructor to help our students learn and practice this sport regularly.

Skating Ring

Skating is a great way for our students to unwind. Our Skating ring provides a great platform for our students to learn and excel in this unique sport.

Kho-Kho ground

Our Kho Kho ground is a spot in the school that helps us relate to our roots. We have a ground and a coach to keep this ancient Indian sport alive.
